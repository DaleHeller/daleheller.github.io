About Me

From photography to ceramics my whole life has been involved in art since my freshman year in highschool. Now it's freshman year of college and things have changed a lot. I'm exploring new mediums like glass, metal, and even coding so I can make this website possible.

I'm from northern Ohio and I've lived here all my life. The Cleveland Institute of Art is where I continue to enjoy Art and explore it more. I have included many of my first semsester works so feel free ot take a break and look around.
